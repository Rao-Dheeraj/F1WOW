📧 EmailJS Setup for F1wow Newsletter

🎯 Goal: Send automatic welcome emails when users subscribe

This setup takes 5 minutes and is 100% free.

1 Create EmailJS Account

Go to EmailJS and sign up with your Google account:

🚀 Open EmailJS.com

Click "Sign Up" → Click "Sign up with Google" → Select your Gmail

2 Add Email Service

After logging in, you need to connect your Gmail:

  1. Click "Add New Service" button
  2. Select "Gmail" from the list
  3. Click "Connect Service Account"
  4. Choose your Google account
  5. Click "Allow" to give EmailJS permission
📋 Copy the Service ID:
After connecting, you'll see a Service ID like: service_abc123def456
Copy this ID - you'll need it later!
3 Create Email Template

Now create the welcome email that subscribers will receive:

  1. Click "Email Templates" in the left menu
  2. Click "Create New Template"
  3. Enter the name: F1wow Welcome

For Subject, enter:

🏁 Welcome to F1wow Newsletter!

For Email Body (HTML), copy and paste this:

<html> <body style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif; background: #0a0a0a; padding: 20px;"> <div style="max-width: 600px; margin: 0 auto; background: #1a1a1a; border-radius: 15px; overflow: hidden;"> <div style="background: #E10600; padding: 30px; text-align: center;"> <h1 style="color: white; margin: 0;">🏎️ F1WOW</h1> </div> <div style="padding: 30px;"> <h2 style="color: #E10600;">Welcome to F1wow! {{to_name}}</h2> <p style="color: #ccc; font-size: 16px;">Thank you for subscribing! You're now part of the F1wow family.</p> <div style="background: #222; padding: 20px; border-radius: 10px; margin: 20px 0;"> <h3 style="color: white; margin-top: 0;">You'll receive:</h3> <ul style="color: #aaa;"> <li>🏁 Latest F1 news and race results</li> <li>🏎️ Driver updates and team news</li> <li>📊 Championship standings</li> <li>🔔 Exclusive content and predictions</li> </ul> </div> <p style="color: #888;">We'll notify you whenever we publish a new article.</p> <a href="https://f1wow.com" style="display: inline-block; background: #E10600; color: white; padding: 12px 30px; text-decoration: none; border-radius: 8px; margin: 20px 0;">Visit F1wow</a> </div> </div> </body> </html>

Add Variables:

  1. Scroll down to "Variables" section
  2. Add variable: to_name
  3. Add variable: to_email
📋 Copy the Template ID:
Click "Save" and you'll see a Template ID like: template_xyz789abc123
Copy this ID!
4 Get Your Public Key
  1. Click your profile picture (top right)
  2. Click "Account"
  3. You'll see Public Key (starts with user_)
📋 Copy the Public Key:
It looks like: user_abc123xyz456
Copy this key!
